Francis E. Dec - A Collection of Works

Greetings! Welcome to the official UG$ Archive of all known works of Francis E. Dec. Listed below is a series of hyperlinks that will direct you to each work, in (as close as possible to) chronological order. Unfortunately, Francis was not very good at dating his works, so in many instances, dates may be inaccurate or plain wrong at worst. If you believe any dates are wrong, please contact us. If you have ended up on this page with little to no knowledge on who Francis E. Dec was or what he did, we suggest you check out the about page to learn more. All transcriptions are intended to be as close to what was written as possible, down to replacements of "S" with "$", "E" with "F", and even the random underlining. If you notice any inconsistencies or (unintentional) typos, please inform us. The titles for each work are determined either by being directly in the text, or being decided upon by trying to capture the essence of the entire work in a few words.
